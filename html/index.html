WHITE RICE
SPROUTED CALIFORNIA RICE
Combine ounces of rice with ounces of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting.
Cook for about 18 minutes.

Ratio: ounces of rice : ounces of water

For slightly al dente rice:
Combine ounces of rice with ounces of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil.
Bring to a boil and stir once to mix.
Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes.
Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes.
Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice:
Increase liquid by ounces and cook time by 5 minutes.

Ratio for al dente: ounces of rice : ounces of water
Ratio for softer: ounces of rice : ounces of water
